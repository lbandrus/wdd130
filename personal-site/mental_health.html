Mental Anxiety

Mental anxiety come in many forms. The more come forms are Intense, excessive and persistent worrying and fear over daily experiences. Symptoms include: raised heart rate, constant worrying that is interfering with daily life, shaking, hyperventilation, feeling of being in danger. Treatments include better sleep, healthy dieting, avoiding caffeine and addictive substances.

Clinical Depression

Clinical depression is a constant depressed mood and loss of interest in activities. Depression usually occurs after loss of a loved one or going through a traumatic experience. Some symptoms include constant sadness, insomnia, loss of appetite and thoughts of suicide. Depression is usually best treated through therapies and medication. Meditation can also help with less depression episodes
