Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help people who are struggling with any form of mental illness to find help. Mental Health is a big issue that needs to be addressed. 1 in 4 people suffer from some kind of mental illness. I want to make a website that connects people to different resources and exercises. People from all walks of life need help with mental needs and it is becoming more of an issue. Having a healthy mind can make all the difference and allow everyone to enjoy life without the mental illness weighing them down. Mental illness can affect our relationships, career, and families. I want to be able to reach out to as many people as possible and help people just like me. I myself have struggled with Anxiety and Depression. I want to share what has helped me and pass it on to other people that are struggling and make the world a better place for all.

Audience

It can be hard to find help when you feel alone. I want to help as many people as I can with mental health because I also have some mental illnesses. I have anxiety and depression. I want people to be able to find the resources that are available. Mental health is important for everyone and any age. It effects men and women. It is also a growing concern.
